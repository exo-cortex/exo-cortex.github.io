pitchescurves

May 26, 2021
idea: creating sound from pure functions. The most elemental wave-forms are sine functions. the pitch of a tone defined over time. Every sound in nature can be deconstructed into a collection of sine functions. A single sine function can only be deconstructed into itself and is therefor called “pure”.
