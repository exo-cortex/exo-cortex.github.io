Marble Quarry

2026-05-17

By accident our vacation home in northern italy happened to be a only few hundred meters away from an impressive marble quarry. Apparently since the ~70s the mountain has slowly been cut up into little giant cubes.

The large construction machines look like toys next to the giant marble cutouts.
